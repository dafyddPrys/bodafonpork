About us

FREE RANGE PORK

direct from the farm to you at your local markets
We use our free range rare breed pork reared in the heart of Anglesey to produce delicious pork joints, chops, sausages, bacon and gammon.

Bodafon Pork was set up in 2013 by Graham Hughes. Our aim is to provide healthy and happy lives for our saddleback pigs and to provide local and sustainably reared meat and produce to local markets. The pigs are reared on a field on the outskirts of Bodafon, along with a collection of beehives and a small selection of other farm animals. Honey is harvested from the bees and can be found sold alongside great pork at local markets around Anglesey and Gwynedd.

Starting at Llangefni market, Bodafon Pork now also sell at Bangor market and Holyhead market every week. We are working hard with other stallholders to revitalise the local markets and bring a great choice of local produce to the local communities.
