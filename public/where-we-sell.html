Where we sell
These are the regular market locations that we attend. We recommend that you keep an eye on twitter for disruption to our attendance.

Llangefni

Thursdays 10am - 4pm

In the car park of Llangefni town hall.

This was our first regular location and we are joined weekly by a fruit stall and curtain dealers, along with Carol's burger van and other regulars.

Bangor

Fridays 10am - 4pm

Bangor hight street

Bangor market has really taken off over the last couple of years. Starting off with a few stalls, there are now more than forty stalls present every Friday and a waiting list with the council for more stalls. Come and see whats on offer at the wide range of stalls, surrounded by the regular offerings of the high street.
